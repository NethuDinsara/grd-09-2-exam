Listening Precision Framework

4 Audio Sections | 40 Questions[cite: 1] | Target 39–40/40 for Band 9

1. Active Word Prediction

Pre-analyze blank spaces during audio prep time. Predict precise parts of speech (noun, verb, adjective) and expected categories (dates, quantities, prices).

2. Distractor Traps

Speakers frequently correct themselves mid-phrase ("We'll begin on Tuesday... actually, let's make it Thursday"). Never record the first data point heard.

3. Plural & Spelling Accuracy

A missing singular/plural noun suffix (-s) causes immediate mark loss. Double-check exact word limits and standard spelling conventions.
