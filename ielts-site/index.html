Master Logic Rules

Questions generally follow paragraph order. Always compare the Question to the Passage, NOT the passage to the question[cite: 2]. Keep an active eye out for synonym language and compound sentence structures (e.g., semicolons `;`)[cite: 2].

Listening Precision Framework

4 Audio Sections | 40 Questions | Band 9 requires 39-40 correct answers

1. Active Word Prediction

Before audio plays, pre-analyze blanks. Predict required parts of speech (noun, verb, adjective) and exact units (date, price, quantity).

2. Distractor Traps

Speakers often correct themselves mid-sentence ("We will meet on Tuesday... wait, no, Thursday"). Never write the first answer you hear.

3. Plural & Spelling Precision

Missing a final "-s" instantly invalidates the response. Verify exact singular vs plural logic and double-check standard British/American spelling.

"Dear Sam ... Warm regards" (Natural contractions allowed).
